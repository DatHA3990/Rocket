Aileron 1 and 3 control rocket yaw
Aileron 2 and 4 control rocket pitch

If in synchronized motion, rocket may control roll.
Function might be programmed in later for roll stabilization.

This is part of the CM.

Program will use 4 guiding ailerons mounted on the CM.
Airlerons will be controled by 4 servo motors.
